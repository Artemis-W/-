The Seven Natural World Wonders of the Material Plane



Yggdrassil

Found within the great kingdom of ___, Yggdrasil, the World Tree, serves as both home to many peoples, and sometimes a gateway into the feywild. A bustling metropolis surrounds it, with people living within it's bark and it's branches, or under the cover of it's roots. Fertile soil covers the land for miles surrounding it, which has helped expand the city and feed the kingdom. But beneath it's roots something curious lies. A passageway surveiled by soldiers because of the dangers beyond. If you follow it you will find yourself in a strange underground land.


Great Reef

Found along the coast to the south of the Ignean Empire, and within the Underwater Republic of ____ this wondrous place is the subject of many a pilgrimage from diverse corners of the world, known both by it's beauty, and the presence of skillful aquatic healers, whose powers are boosted by the latent magic of the reef. Also notable is the relation it holds to Evanescet isle, as it can often be seen moving near the edges of the reef only to suddenly dissappear within the hour. Also within the reef, underwater settlements are commonplace, living off the algae and animals that are abundant in the area.


Dorgheshuun Volcano

A magestic centerpiece of the Kingdom of Dorgheshan, where the forges of Grahkar and the springs of Khealth lie, places where the most skilled of orcish artisans and healers work. Many travel to see the magnificent river Vhrilo, which shines in rainbow colours as it passes over the glowing cristaline sediment that was formed by the mystical fires of the volcano. Dormant for almost a millenia, the volcano is thought to have been frozen by Nerissa, where pyra was put laid to heal after a great battle that left her scarred.


Mystical Falls

Hidden within the jungles of Kraidsun the Mystical falls are the origin point to three rivers, those being Necro, Iluno and Vento, which are the main source of water for all the creatures that inhabit the rainforest. The falls are surrounded by floating islands, levitated by crystals of great power, from which water pours down. These cristals can't be found anywhere else, but also do not seem artificial in nature, but rather the result of divine action in the past.


Mount Karuulm

Standing as the tallest peak on the realm and covered in bright vegetation all the way to the top, this place is magical only in subtle ways. Despite the fact that air should not be present at this height, creatures can breathe normally when around the Cherry trees that grow at Mount Karuulm, and so many a settlement has formed in this unlikely place, taking advantage of the region's topography to defend themselves from external threats.
