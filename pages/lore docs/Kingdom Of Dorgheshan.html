Recent History of the Kingdom

The history of Dorgheshan is fraught with violence, enacted both by the orcs themselves toward each other, and towards other countries. This cycle however, was broken half a century ago by a great ruler, ____ the unifier, who, through both strategic and tactical knowhow, as well as a deep diplomatic prowess, unified all the orcish tribes under one banner, and established a system of education, a strong military and treaties between region leaders, as well as with bordering countries, that would serve to establish trade routes, and punish violence.

A golden age followed, which continues to this day under the rule of her daughter, ___, which has seen the devolopment of many cities, temples, mines, and the recently founded school of magic, which boasts fascinating arcane architecture. It takes the shape of a castle, but you are always just one door away from your destination. This is partly because of ____'s best friend's fascination with the arcane, being an experienced magical user, which combined with her knowledge of architecture, led her to build the place she would have liked to study when she was a child.
