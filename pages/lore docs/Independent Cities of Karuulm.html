Recent History of the Kingdom

 The history is Karuulm is one of hope in desperate times, as the city-states were formed in response to corrupt people holding power on it's bordering states, which led opressed people to seek freedom and escape their opressors. In recent times this still holds true, people who flee their homelands for one reason or another, are able to make new lives in in Karuulm, and many communities of people of similar heritage band together and hold each other up.  With the rise to power of the new chancelor of Kourend, the tensions run high, as his oposition, who had been in governance for a decade and a half, are making underhanded moves to discredit tbe new government.
